Discipline: Criterium

## Athlete Goal

- Perform strongly in short high-intensity races (30–90 min)
- Improve anaerobic capacity (W')
- Improve repeatability of short efforts
- Improve sprint and acceleration

## Training Focus Priority

1. repeatability
2. VO2max
3. anaerobic capacity (W')

## Discipline-Specific Rules (CRITICAL)

- prioritize short high-intensity intervals
- use:
  → 30/30
  → 40/20
  → microbursts

- include sprint efforts (Z6–Z7)

- simulate race dynamics:
  → accelerations out of corners
  → attacks
  → incomplete recovery

- avoid:
  → long steady rides as primary focus
  → long threshold intervals as dominant stimulus
