Discipline: Climber

## Athlete Goal

- Increase FTP
- Improve long-duration climbing performance (60–90 minutes)

## Training Focus Priority

1. threshold (FTP)
2. aerobic durability
3. VO2max

## Discipline-Specific Rules (CRITICAL)

- prioritize steady-state performance
- focus on long Z3–Z4 intervals
- long rides should be steady (upper Z2)
- avoid early anaerobic spikes
