Discipline: Road Race

## Athlete Goal

- Perform strongly in road races (2–5 hours)
- Sustain high power with repeated surges
- Improve FTP and aerobic durability
- Improve repeatability under fatigue

## Training Focus Priority

1. aerobic durability
2. threshold (FTP)
3. repeatability

## Discipline-Specific Rules (CRITICAL)

- include variable intensity in long rides
- simulate race dynamics:
  → surges (Z4–Z5)
  → hills
  → attacks

- combine:
  → steady Z2 base
  → stochastic load on top

- long rides should NOT be purely steady
